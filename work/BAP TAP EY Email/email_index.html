FSO BAP TAP
Digital Innovation Challenge


FSO Digital Practice invites BAP and TAP Staff 1s, Staff 2s and Senior 1s (as of 10/1/2016) to participate in the BAP TAP Digital Innovation Challenge to learn about digital topics and solve a problem with core User Experience skills.


Register Now!

Challenge Overview

The challenge will involve breaking into assigned teams to resolve a problem by learning core User Experience skills. Ultimately, the challenge will require top teams to present to EY executive leadership, who will then determine a final winner.


New for 2017

As many of you may know our Spring Challenge was a huge success, which included over 100 BAP TAPs across four major cities. Not to mention our final judges included Anthony Caterino, Peter Davis, Angie Cercone, and Dan Costa (Editor and Chief, PC Mag) to name a few.

This time around, we’re going bigger. 2017's Challenge will aim to solve a problem statement from one of our clients! This means your presentation has the potential to be delivered not only to EY Senior Leadership, but to our clients Executive Leadership, bringing the external exposure of this challenge to a whole new level.

Learn Digital Skills

Over the course of the project, you will learn, via guided classroom and virtual learnings, about digital topics including:

User Research

Learn methodology, data analysis, design recommendations
 

User Journeys

Develop personas and user journeys

Storytelling

Create a focused memorable message
 

Wireframing

Practice rapid sketching and evaluation techniques

Prototyping

Leverage prototying to test and explain
 

Usability Testing

Testing protocals and data analysis

Requirements

  • Eligible applicants are BAP & TAP Staff 1s, Staff 2s and Senior 1s (as of 10/1/2016) located in New York, Charlotte, McLean and San Francisco
  • Participation in this challenge will require an approximate commitment of four to eight hours per week for the duration of the challenge (variable depending on week) and is expected to be in addition to your current engagement assignments
  • The challenge will run from January through April
